Euro Cup

The UEFA European Championship, commonly known as the Euro Cup, is the main football competition for European nations.

Facts About the Euro Cup

  • Founded: 1960
  • Number of Teams: 24
  • Current Champion: Italy (2020)
  • Most Titles: Germany and Spain (3 titles each)

Top 5 Euro Cup Goal Scorers

  1. Cristiano Ronaldo – 14 goals
  2. Michel Platini – 9 goals
  3. Alan Shearer – 7 goals
  4. Antoine Griezmann – 6 goals
  5. Ruud van Nistelrooy – 6 goals

Most Successful Teams

  1. Germany – 3 titles
  2. Spain – 3 titles
  3. France – 2 titles
  4. Italy – 2 titles
  5. Netherlands – 1 title
